The Location

The Ceremony at Keele Chapel
Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Keele University campus, Keele Chapel provides the perfect setting for our wedding ceremony. With its stunning architecture, serene atmosphere, and historic charm, the Chapel is a beautiful place for us to exchange vows and begin our journey together. Surrounded by the tranquility of nature and the majesty of its design, Keele Chapel offers an unforgettable backdrop for such a meaningful moment.

The Celebration at Keele Hall
Following the ceremony, we invite you to join us at Keele Hall for an evening of celebration and joy. This magnificent stately home, with its grand architecture and elegant grounds, exudes timeless sophistication and warmth. The Hall’s sweeping rooms, adorned with both classic and contemporary touches, will serve as the perfect setting for our reception. Surrounded by beautiful gardens, this venue promises an atmosphere of luxury and romance, making it the ideal place to continue the festivities with great food, dancing, and laughter.

We are thrilled to celebrate our special day at these exceptional locations, and we hope you enjoy the beauty and charm of Keele as much as we do.

Getting There

By Car

Parking is available at the venue. Use postcode ST5 5BG for your GPS, and take junction 15 on the M6.

Please allow extra time for traffic on the day.

By Train

The nearest station is Stoke-on-Trent, 15 minutes away by taxi.

Frequent services are available from major cities.

By Taxi

Uber & Local taxis are available.ROSEVILLE

We recommend booking in advance during peak hours.

By Plane

The nearest airport is Manchester International, 45 minutes by car.

Direct trains are also available from the airport to Stoke-on-Trent.

By Bus

Frequent bus services run to the venue from the town center and nearby areas.

Check local bus timetables for exact routes and times.

By Foot

The venue is a 10-minute walk from the Marriott Courtyard on Campus.

Follow the signs on University Drive to reach the event location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a plus one?
 Unfortunately, if we’ve not specified a plus one on your invite, we are not able to accommodate additional guests.

Can I bring my child?
 Although we love your little ones, we sadly cannot accommodate children. We appreciate you making arrangements so that you can celebrate our big day with us.

Can I bring my dog?
 No, do not bring your dog (or any other pet) please.

What is the dress code?
 Dress Code: Formal Attire
We kindly request that our guests join us in dressing to impress for our special day.
Ladies: Elegant dresses, gowns, or chic separates.
Gentlemen: Lounge suits, ties, or formal attire.
We’d love for you to feel comfortable and stylish while celebrating with us, so please let the season inspire your look. Hats and fascinators are optional but always a wonderful touch!

What time should I arrive and what time does it end?
 The ceremony will start at 12:45, so please arrive for midday.
The venue will close sharply at 01:30am, so please ensure your taxi is booked if you are not stopping on site.
