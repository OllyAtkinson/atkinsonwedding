About Us

Our journey began in 2016, when we were elected as Social Secretary and Treasurer of the Keele University Swimming Team. What started as committee roles quickly blossomed into something far more special. Olly’s cheeky Yorkshire charm and Gabby’s bubbly Essex fun were the perfect mix, and from there, we became inseparable. As best friends and partners, not a day has passed without laughter and love.
Over the past eight years, we’ve made countless memories, travelled to over 30 countries, and bought our first home together. Now, we’re ready to embark on the next chapter of our lives as the Atkinsons.
We’re thrilled to celebrate our love and the start of this exciting new chapter with all of you. Our journey has been filled with adventure, laughter, and unforgettable moments – and we can’t wait to create even more memories together on our wedding day.

The Location

The Ceremony at Keele Chapel
Nestled in the heart of the university campus, Keele Chapel holds a special place in our memories. From late-night “tea and toast” after SU nights out, to the unforgettable moments of our graduation ceremonies. There couldn’t be a more perfect setting for us to exchange our vows and begin this next chapter of our journey together.

The Celebration at Keele Hall
After the ceremony, we’d love for you to join us at Keele Hall for an afternoon filled with celebration and joy. Dating back to 1851, this stunning stately home is not only the site of the legendary ‘Freshers Gate,’ but also where we celebrated the swim team's victory in 2017 when we took home ‘Club of the Year.’
The stories embedded in its walls, its grand architecture and elegant grounds will set the ideal stage for our reception.

We are thrilled to celebrate our special day at these exceptional locations, and we hope you enjoy the beauty and charm of Keele as much as we do.

Getting There

By Car

Parking is free across Keele Campus at weekends. Use postcode ST5 5NN for your GPS, and take junction 15 on the M6.

Please allow extra time for traffic on the day.

By Train

The nearest station is Stoke-on-Trent, 15 minutes away by taxi.

Frequent services are available from major cities.

By Taxi

Uber & Local taxis (Roseville, 01782 631234) are available.

We recommend booking in advance during peak hours.

By Plane

The nearest airport is Manchester International, 45 minutes by car.

Direct trains are also available from the airport to Stoke-on-Trent.

By Bus

Frequent bus services run to the venue from the town center and nearby areas.

Check local bus timetables for exact routes and times.

By Foot

The venue is a 10-minute walk from the Marriott Courtyard on Campus.

Follow the signs on University Drive to reach the event location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a plus one?

Unfortunately, if we’ve not specified a plus one on your invite, we are not able to accommodate additional guests.



Can I bring my child?

Although we love your little ones, we sadly cannot accommodate children. We appreciate you making arrangements so that you can celebrate our big day with us.



Can I bring my dog?

No, do not bring your dog (or any other pet) please.



What is the dress code?

Dress Code: Formal Attire
We kindly request that our guests join us in dressing to impress for our special day.
Ladies: Elegant dresses, gowns, or chic separates.
Gentlemen: Lounge suits, ties, or formal attire.
We’d love for you to feel comfortable and stylish while celebrating with us, so please let the season inspire your look. Hats and fascinators are optional but always a wonderful touch!



What time should I arrive and what time does it end?

Please arrive to Keele Chapel by 12:30 for a prompt ceremony start.
Keele Hall will close sharply at 01:30, so please ensure your taxi is booked if you are not staying on site.



Can I take photos during the ceremony?

There will be a designated photographer and videographer, but feel free to snap the day as you please. Do share your memories with Gabriella & Oliver after the event.



When is the deadline to RSVP?

We kindly ask that you RSVP by 28th February so that we can confirm numbers with the venue. We will endeavour to accommodate responses after this point, however cannot guarantee it will be possible.



Can I bring my own confetti?

You are welcome to bring your own confetti, however the venue kindly ask for this to be biodegradable to protect the beauty of the venue.



What do I do if I have a question?

Feel free to call or message either Gabriella or Oliver.
